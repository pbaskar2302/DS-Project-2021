Assignment Rules

  1. Professional games should have at least 4 referees
  2. Mid level games should have at least 3 referees
  3. Low level recreational games should have at least 1 referees
  4. Referees must be experienced in that specific position when playing in non-recreational games
  5. Prioritize referees with high skill when playing for important games such as finals
